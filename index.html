No 1    PUMPKIN SOUP

 

 

1 kilo pumpkin

1 onion

2 rashers bacon (optional)

1 litre chicken stock (1-2 stock cubes)

salt and pepper to taste.

 

 

  1. Remove rind from bacon and roughly chop.
  2. Peel and roughly chop onion.
  3. Peel and roughly chop pumpkin.
  4. Heat saucepan pan and add bacon to saucepan and heat stirring constantly for 2 minutes. (No fat is needed as bacon provides fat)
  5. Add onions and cook till they are soft but not brown.
  6. Add pumpkin, stir well, cover and allow to “sweat” for 5 minutes on a low heat.
  7. Add stock and cook covered for 20-25 minutes till vegetables are cooked.
  8. Puree soup until smooth.
  9. Reheat season to taste and serve.

 

 



 

No 2    LEEK AND POTATO SOUP

 


3 leeks

1 onion

2 large potatoes

2 rashers bacon (optional)

 

1 litre chicken stock (1- 2 stock cubes)

salt and pepper to taste

2 extra potatoes

 

 

  1. Trim the top and bottom of the leeks, remove outer leaves, wash well.
  2. Slice leeks thinly.
  3. Peel and roughly chop onion.
  4. Peel and slice potatoes.
  5. Remove rind from bacon and roughly chop.
  6. Heat saucepan pan and add bacon to saucepan and heat stirring constantly for 2 minutes. (No fat is needed as bacon provides fat)
  7. Add onion, leeks and potatoes stir well, cover and allow to “sweat” for 5 minutes on a low heat.
  8. Add stock and cook covered for 15-20 minutes till vegetables are soft.
  9. Puree soup until it is smooth.
  10. Peel and dice extra potatoes into 2cm cubes. Place in a saucepan of boiling water and cook till soft, approx. 10 minutes. Drain well.
  11. Return the soup to the heat add the drained potato cubes, season to taste and heat to serve.
